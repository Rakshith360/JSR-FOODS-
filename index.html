From Golden Fields to Your Kitchen

The Chakki Fresh Promise

At Golden Grain, we believe that the best flour comes from the best wheat and the most authentic process. Our journey begins by sourcing premium Sharbati wheat, known for its sweet taste and high nutritional value, directly from the sun-kissed fields of Central India.

We employ the traditional 'chakki' grinding method, where slow-moving stone grinders gently mill the grains. This process preserves the natural dietary fibers and nutrients, ensuring our atta is not only fresh but also wholesome. The result is a soft, aromatic flour that makes perfect, fluffy chapatis every time.

Our Meticulous Process

1. Wheat Sourcing

We select only the finest, naturally grown wheat grains from trusted farmers to ensure superior quality.

2. Triple Cleaning

The grains undergo a rigorous three-step cleaning process to remove all impurities, dust, and stones.

3. Stone Grinding

Our traditional chakkis grind the wheat slowly, retaining the bran, endosperm, and germ for maximum nutrition.

4. Hygienic Packaging

The fresh atta is packed in moisture-proof, food-grade bags to lock in the freshness and aroma.
